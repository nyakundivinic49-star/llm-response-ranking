# AI Response Comparison Case Study

## Evaluation Scenario

### User Query

> Explain diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA), including its causes and the symptoms commonly associated with the condition.

---

## Candidate Response 1

Diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA) is a serious medical emergency that develops when the body does not have enough insulin available. As a result, fat is broken down for energy, producing ketones that accumulate in the bloodstream.

Common signs and symptoms include:

* Excessive thirst
* Frequent urination
* Nausea and vomiting
* Abdominal discomfort
* Fruity-smelling breath
* Rapid or deep breathing
* Fatigue and weakness
* Altered mental status or confusion

Management typically requires urgent medical treatment involving intravenous fluids, insulin therapy, and electrolyte replacement.

---

## Candidate Response 2

DKA occurs when blood glucose levels become elevated. Thirst is generally the only important symptom, and most people recover by resting and increasing water intake without requiring urgent medical treatment.
